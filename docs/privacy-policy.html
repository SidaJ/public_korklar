Privacy Policy for KorkortQuiz

Last updated: March 22, 2026

KorkortQuiz respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you when you use the app.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Email address, used to create and manage your account and help you sign in.
  • Display name, used to personalize your profile inside the app.
  • Quiz progress and activity, used to save your daily tests, answers, results, and subscription status.
  • Subscription-related information, used to manage premium features through payment providers.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data such as health information, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, or precise location through this app.

How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Authenticate users.
  • Save quiz history and progress.
  • Provide premium subscription features.
  • Improve app functionality and reliability.
  • Respond to support or account issues.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party service providers to operate the app, including:

  • Supabase for authentication, database, and backend services.
  • Stripe for subscription and payment-related processing.
  • Resend or similar email delivery services for account-related emails.

These providers may process personal information only as needed to provide their services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers that help us run the app, process subscriptions, deliver emails, and support core app functionality.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Data is transmitted over secure connections where supported by our service providers.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Choices

  • Update your profile information where available.
  • Stop using the app at any time.
  • Request deletion of your account or associated personal data by contacting support.

Children's Privacy

KorkortQuiz is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law to use the service independently.

International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, depending on where our service providers operate.
